This service can be used without registration or login details. We do not store any personal information on our servers. However, the following information may be automatically generated and collected during service usage:

The Service uses 'cookies' to store and retrieve usage information to provide personalized services.

This Service may display advertisements via Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

All code (HTML, CSS, JS) entered into the editor is processed and stored only within the user's browser (Local Storage) and is not transmitted to or stored on the operator's server.
